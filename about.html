About Us

Since 1996, Tex Arts has served the garment procurement needs of home garment / textile fabrics / clothes and made-up importers from Pakistan, with access to a wide range of items. Tex Art guarantees that the goods you purchase are sourced and of outstanding quality from reputable businesses.

We always followed a tradition of Quality and guaranteed satisfaction for our customers. Likewise, we have made our business to provide the customers with efficient services and quality products on our arranged production units at competitive prices.

Ever since it began, our dedication to success and creativity has been a significant factor in the growth of the business. Our company's goal is to prioritise high quality goods. The happiness of our consumers is our greatest strength. At any point of development, from knitting to final packaging, we ensure that the standard is according to the buyer's specification.

Tex Art has a mandate to sell the products of the well-known Textile Mills of Pakistan, which helps us to supply our customers with premium items at highly competitive prices. We a Tex Arts trusts in our customers' continued growth and acts as a team with them.
