Who Are We ?

Prosperous textile experts consistently have an artistic view with intimate knowledge of the specialized materials of manufacturing and the properties of fiber, yarn, and dyes. With a drawing or watercolor sketch of the finished design, proposals for both woven and printed materials frequently begin. Generally, sketches of samples of woven material were deciphered on peculiar types of so-called point papers that were used by the weavers to set up their weaving setups.
